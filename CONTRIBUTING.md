# Contributing

Contributions welcome. This list covers **runtime governance of AI agents** — policy enforcement, audit trails, access control, cost management, and compliance tooling for systems where AI agents operate in production.

## What belongs here

- Open-source tools for enforcing governance at agent runtime
- Enterprise platforms for AI risk, governance, and compliance
- Standards and regulatory frameworks with direct implementation impact
- Audit, observability, and cost tracking tools for agent systems
- Security tooling and threat models for agentic AI
- Architecture patterns for safe production agent deployment

## What does not belong here

- General AI safety or alignment research
- AI ethics principles or governance manifestos (without tooling)
- Courses or tutorials on building agents (without governance focus)
- Vendor marketing content without a substantive free tier or open-source component

## How to submit

1. Open an issue with the title `[Submission]: Tool Name`
2. Include: name, URL, one-sentence description, which section it belongs in, and why it meets the scope criteria
3. PRs are also accepted — add your entry in alphabetical order within the relevant section

## Quality bar

- Must be publicly accessible (open-source or free tier)
- Must be actively maintained (commit within 12 months, or standards body with active update cycle)
- Description must be factual — no marketing language, no unverified performance claims
- If you are the author of a tool, disclose that in the issue
